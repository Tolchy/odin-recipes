Chicken Salad

Recipe Overview

Everyone needs a good, basic chicken salad recipe in their collection of fool-proof dishes. Homemade chicken salad is quick and easy to make with just a handful of everyday ingredients, and is perfect for warm weather picnics, potlucks, lunches, or light dinners.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped chicken
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1/2 cup slivered almonds
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions

  1. Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium heat, shaking frequently.
  2. Mix together mayo and lemon juice.
  3. Pour mixture into a bowl with checken, celery, almonds and toss.
  4. Voila! You're a newly minted chef ;)
