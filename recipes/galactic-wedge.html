Galactic Cheese Wedge

Recipe Overview

One cannot miss out on taking a bite from the coveted Cheese en Space

Ingredients

  • 1 Cow
  • 1 microgravity chamber (tesla model II)
  • 1 starship
  • Lots of mouths
  • 1 drop of chromium nitrate

Directions

  1. Put people and cow on starship, fly starship to space.
  2. Put cow in microgravity chamber and force launch with pinpoint accuracy into chromium nitrate drop.
  3. Hope that it's a full moo-n.
  4. Then devour the newly created cheese monstrosity.
